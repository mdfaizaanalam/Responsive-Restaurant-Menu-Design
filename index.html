About Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen

From a single restaurant in 1972 to over 4,300 locations worldwide, Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen has been on an incredible journey. We began with a dream to serve the best fried chicken, and today, our bold, soulful flavors rooted in Louisiana traditions continue to turn any meal into a celebration. Let’s party NOLA style!

Our Heritage

Founded by Al Copeland in 1972, Popeyes® revolutionized fast food with New Orleans-style fried chicken. Our menu, inspired by Cajun and Creole flavors, features spicy chicken, chicken tenders, fried shrimp, and other regional delights. With over 2,700 locations globally, we’re proud to be a Louisiana legend, committed to authentic, high-quality food that brings people together.
